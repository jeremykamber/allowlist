# AllowList - Chrome Web Store Listing

## Product Details

### Title
AllowList

### Summary
Block everything by default. Only allow sites you add. Manage lists and switch between them with keyboard shortcuts.

### Description
Stay focused and boost your productivity with AllowList—a powerful allowlist-based site blocker for Chrome.

By default, AllowList blocks all websites. You decide which sites deserve your attention by adding them to your allowlist. Perfect for students, professionals, traders, writers, and anyone serious about minimizing distractions.

#### FEATURES:
- Block everything by default, whitelist only what you need
- Quick-add current site or domain with one click
- Manage multiple allowlists for different contexts (Work, Learning, Deep Focus, etc.)
- Pre-built templates for common use cases
- Keyboard shortcuts for power users (Cmd/Ctrl+Shift+L to add, Cmd/Ctrl+Shift+K to switch lists)
- Detailed analytics dashboard to track your focus patterns
- Cross-device sync via Google Account
- Free and open-source

Perfect for anyone who wants maximum control over their browsing habits.
